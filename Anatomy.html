Anatomy of a Rifle

The back of the rifle is called the butt, theres a few tosses you do from the butt

The strap on the underside is called the strap, theres a few tricks and tosses you do from the strap

The bolt is the silver (or black) part in the middle and from what I know theres not a lot of tosses from that part of the rifle

The rivet between the bolt and the butt is called the neck, you do some tosses while holding from here

The long part at the front is the nose, you do some maneuvers with nose and you also hold the nose for some tosses


Overall, rifle seems to be more toss focused than a lot of equipments, being clunkier than saber but smaller and less eye catching than flag you need real explosive power to be eye catching

Anatomy of a Sabre

The handle of the sabre is know as the hilt (or the handle)

The front of the hilt is your guard this is where you do most of your tosses(I don't do sabre)

The blade of the sabre is called (you guessed it) the blade, you toss from the blade a lot too

The top of the blade is the tip, I don't really know of anything you'd do from here


Overall, sabre is way more of moving your body and dance than other equipments but don't get it twisted this thing flies when tossed

Anatomy of a Flag

The top of the pole is called is the tip

The area where the silk connects to the flag is called the sleeve

The pieces of tape throughout the pole are your 'cheaters' or just your tapes

Theres two tapes under the sleeve

the bottom of your pole is called the crutch tip


While flag is still about body movement I find that it hindges on tosses a lot more than some of your other equipments
